(From Wikipedia) Polypedilum pembai, is a dipteran in the family Chironomidae (non-biting midges). It is desiccation tolerant, making it an ideal species for comparison with Polypedilum vanderplanki.

Assembly

Genome assembly obtained through Illumina DNA-Seq reads. Specifics can be obtained from the genome paper.

Annotation

Gene annotation obtained with augustus using all RNA-Seq data from Kikawada/Gusev lab.
